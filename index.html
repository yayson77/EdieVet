Meet Dr. Edie Hornberger & Our Dedicated Team

Dr. Edie Hornberger is a passionate and experienced veterinarian dedicated to providing personalized and gentle care to every animal she meets. With a deep love for all creatures great and small, Dr. Hornberger founded PawPromise with the mission of fostering strong bonds between pets and their families through comprehensive healthcare.

Our compassionate veterinary technicians and support staff are committed to making your pet's visit as comfortable and stress-free as possible. We believe in clear communication and working together with pet owners to achieve the best health outcomes.
